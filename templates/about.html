Why Afyatoon Exists

Vision

An empowered community with correct socio-behavioural practice information and knowledge towards better health practice obtained by using entertaining visual animations.

Mission

To bring informative and empowering community education to the communities’ doorsteps, inspiring and impacting Behavioural changes towards good health practices through story based health information presented as visual animations

Core Values

Embracing the art of Co-creation, creativity, simplicity and content diversity.
