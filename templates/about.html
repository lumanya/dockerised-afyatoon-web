Our Projects

Get to know Afyatoon projects

Afyatoon

Afyatoon is a media-based innovative project; it uses visual art technology to present health information in a story based, educative and entertaining manner to the targeted audience on digital basis through social media platforms, interactive websites and local community cinema showcasing.

Learn more

NURU; The fight Against FGM

An educational and empowering tool for collective youth engagement to fight FGM.
Nuru, to mean light and derived from the name of a main character from a 3D animation series against female genital mutilation (FGM) is an educational and empowering tool for collective youth engagement to fight FGM.

Learn more

Journalists; AMR communication frontliners.

This is an in-depth Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) course for journalism students and young journalists across the country, it is designed to be a three stages AMR course; Beginner knowledge on AMR, an intermediate and advance knowledge coupled with an in-depth systematic nurturing program and AMR based content dissemination among the Tanzanian community.
